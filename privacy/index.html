Privacy policy

Last updated: 21 April 2026

This policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and your rights under UK data protection law (UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018).

Who we are

Kell's Kolour Shop is an auto body repair business based at 344a Glen Road, Belfast, BT11 8ER. For any privacy query, contact us at kellskolourshop@gmail.com or call 07525 645242.

What we collect

When you contact us through the website form, WhatsApp, or by phone, we collect:

  • Your name and phone number
  • Any message or details you share about your vehicle and the damage
  • Your email address if you provide one

When you visit the website, we collect anonymous analytics data (pages viewed, approximate location, device type) via Google Analytics — only if you consent via the cookie banner.

How we use it

We use your contact details only to:

  • Respond to your enquiry about a repair
  • Arrange the repair, courtesy car, and insurance claim if you book a job
  • Follow up after the work if needed (e.g. a warranty question)

We do not sell or share your information with third parties for marketing. We do not send marketing emails unless you've specifically asked us to.

How we store it

Enquiries submitted through the website are sent to our customer management system (GoHighLevel) which stores them securely on our behalf. We keep enquiries for up to two years so we can help you with follow-up questions or warranty claims, after which they're deleted.

Cookies & analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. This sets cookies on your device only if you click "Accept" on the cookie banner. You can change your choice at any time by clearing your cookies and reloading the site. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Ask what personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct or delete your data
  • Withdraw consent to marketing at any time
  • Complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk)

To exercise any of these rights, email kellskolourshop@gmail.com or call 07525 645242.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we'll update the date at the top of the page. Significant changes will be flagged on the homepage.
