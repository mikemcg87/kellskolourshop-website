Why Belfast drivers come to us

Most body shops want to hand you the claim paperwork and a phone number. We do the opposite — we pick it up from you and sort it. That matters when you've just had a bad morning, you're still shaken, and the last thing you want is an hour on the phone to your insurer.

We're also insurance approved, which means claims go through smoothly. No arguments with assessors, no "we don't deal with that repairer" nonsense, no delays. Your insurer pays the bill, we do the work, you get your car back.

And the £250 cash back isn't a gimmick. For most drivers, that wipes out your excess entirely — so a non-fault claim becomes fully-free, and even a fault claim becomes near-free.
